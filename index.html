About Davion

Born in Danville, Illinois, and raised in Champaign-Urbana, Davion Williams blends musical excellence, ministry leadership, and civic engagement to strengthen the communities he serves.

He holds a Bachelor of Music Performance from Central State University and a Master of Music in Performance and Literature from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

Davion serves Mt. Calvary Missionary Baptist Church as Director of Music and Media and supports outreach efforts including Dare to Care, Stone Creek’s Big Give, and the MLK Celebration Community Choir.
